How To Play

You get six tries to guess the hidden five-letter word by reading the graph.

  • The white curve is the answer line you are trying to match.
  • Each of the five dots stands for one letter, going left to right.
  • Low on the graph means the letter is closer to A. High on the graph means the letter is closer to Z.
  • When you guess a word, that word appears below the graph and its colored line is drawn on the chart.
  • If a letter is exactly right in the right spot, that box turns green. There is no yellow hint in Curvele.
