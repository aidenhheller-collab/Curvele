Guess the hidden five-letter word by reading the graph shape. Compare your colored guess lines against the white answer curve and solve it in six tries.

How To Play

You have six tries to guess the hidden five-letter word by reading the shape of the graph.

  • The white line is the answer curve you are trying to decode.
  • Each of the five points matches one letter position from left to right.
  • Low on the graph means the letter is closer to A. High on the graph means the letter is closer to Z.
  • When you guess a word, the guess appears below the graph and its line is added on top of the chart for comparison.
  • Only exact position matches turn green in the guess row and on the answer graph. There is no yellow hint in this game.
